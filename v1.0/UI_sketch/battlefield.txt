The battle field consists of the main battle screen, a upper Menu bar, a mini thumbnail of entire map, a Data field on left.

Mean bar consists of: information of resources, game status, menu.
1.Information of resources displays current player's resources and their ammount.
2.Game status displays current game time since started, total number of turns, which player's which turn and which phase.
3.Menu shows a side-down menu after click, and it will allow user to save, load, options, exit current game.

Map will display a thumbnail of entire map, current view of map is displayed inside a square on thumbnail. And player can click on thumbnail to bring their view to destination point.

Main battle screen shows part of the whole map, and this is the place where players can operate with their units and territories. Different players' units will be marked with a color assigned at the lobby.
To do operation on a unit, player need to click on that unit and a circle with operation will appear. Player can now select what operation they want on this unit like "attack" or "merge".

Data will display information about current selected unit. If a villager is selected, how many gold it cost each turn, how many gold he mined, how many buildings it construced etc.
